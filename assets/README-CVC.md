Individual - Sprint 02

User Story 1:
As a Character I want to have at least a temporary sprite so that I am visually represented in the game.
https://github.com/College-of-the-Redwoods/PlagueRats/issues/3

I have always been aware of pixel art and sprites, however, I'd never attempted to make any before.
I researched how sprites are made and the various sprite making programs available. 
Brandon James Greer has excellent videos on the subject. He uses Photoshop but there are dedicated apps for pixel art creation. 

I experimented with Pixaki on my iPad and created a handful of temporary sprites.
See the assets folder for a few examples: 
blkrat01.png, 
blkrat02.png, 
dressedrat.png, 
ladyrat.png
smrat.png

I also experimented with animating sprites, as we will eventually want dynamic sprites.

There are a few other open source pixel art apps I am going to try, but I'm liking Pixaki. 
I was worried pixel art would be tedious like pointillism, but it's not too bad.
I'm getting the hang of the limited nature of the media and leaning into it.
